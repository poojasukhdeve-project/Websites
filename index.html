    Our Journey

    Ms. Alka Ramesh Mohod and Ms. Nanda Kisanrao Mokalkar both have been friends for 10 years. They have been working in the grocery and vegetable business for 10 years. There are 3,000 farmers connected with them to deliver their groceries and vegetables to the national markets. We have eliminated the chain of intermediaries and the delivery of goods directly from farmers, these things save us time and money. We focus on offering products at lower prices than the market with a quality guarantee. Also, we have to work international level. we not only work nationally but also internationally to deliver our products. Now we have started our journey to start ATS International Exports to connect with the international market and farmers.

    About Us

     “The secret of doing business is not just doing business, but also being creative with quality products that provide excellent customer service.” ATS INTERNATIONAL EXPORTS operates in India since 2021. We mainly deal with fruits and vegetables, cereals and legumes, spices, nuts, organic food, and some other products. Wheat flour, etc. We focus on quality, quantity, variety, precision, services, and relationship. We have a strong team that works with enthusiasm and courage to fulfill the enduring traditions of our company. Our products are the best in the industry as they are specially manufactured in accordance with national and international quality management standards. We are one of the most listed companies in the sector. To ensure the highest possible satisfaction to buyers, we ensure that the delivery schedule is properly adhered to.
